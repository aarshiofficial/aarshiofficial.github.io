Act I

Who We Are

AARSHI envisions the promotion of classical theatre acting and various forms of performance — while bringing together cultures from across the country onto a single stage.

Beyond the stage lights, AARSHI stands as a family — a space built on trust, collaboration, learning, and shared memories. We nurture individuals both as performers and as people: building confidence, discipline, teamwork, and artistic freedom.

With every production, AARSHI seeks to inspire, provoke thought, and celebrate the timeless magic of theatre.

Through the Years

Our Productions

Every year, one story. Every story, a new world.

2022
Annual Drama Production

Sab Changa Si

AARSHI's first full-scale annual production — a heartfelt exploration of ordinary lives and extraordinary resilience, performed before a packed auditorium at IISER Kolkata.

2023
Annual Drama Production · IICM Gold 🥇

Andh-Dwand

A powerful narrative navigating conflict, identity, and ambiguity — earning AARSHI its first IICM Group Play Gold. The production marked a turning point in the club's artistic ambition.

▶ Watch on YouTube
2024
Annual Drama Production · IICM Gold 🥇

PankhJhat

An evocative piece on flight, freedom, and the weight of expectations — brought to life with rich visual storytelling and a cast of over 30. AARSHI won its second consecutive IICM Gold and was crowned "Thespian Titans".

2025
Annual Drama Production · IICM Gold 🥇

Lakeer-e-Kabaddi

A bold, energetic production weaving sport, politics, and human boundaries into a single breathtaking stage experience. Three consecutive IICM Golds cemented AARSHI's legacy in inter-IISER theatre.

Annual Drama Production · Coming Soon

Untitled 2026

The next chapter is being written. Auditions, workshops, and the full production are on the horizon — stay tuned via our Instagram for the big reveal.
